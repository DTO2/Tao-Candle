Sustainability

By purchasing a Tao Candle we plant a tree for you

We wanted to start a feasible business that promotes the growth of nature and goes against the current norm of profit first environmental health second. Some buisness's are set up in such a way that things like deforestation is beneficial towawrds their incentives. Tao Candle aims to create products in harmony with the sustainability of the planet. like many other sustainable companies that now recognise the climate crisis we're going to plant a tree for every candle we sell. Through social media we hope this idea contributes towards living a greener life.

Our Suppliers

Essential Oil

We make sure we source our essential oil directly from the plant field farmers of which the plants grow. To many essential oil companies claim there "essential oil" is "pure" yet their ingredient list contains many addictives. Our suppliers use steam distillation to extract the oil from the plant or natural products.
